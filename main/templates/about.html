Join Dancing Together using your Spotify Premium account and tune into a music station for real-time, curated music by user DJ's like you. Let your souls dance together no matter the distance!

Dancing Together was created in 2018 by Bob Gardner and Nicole Ansbacher to listen to music together while being in a long distance relationship. This project was also inspired by working in an office environment where we wanted to listen to music together without disrupting others.

Dancing Together is open source
